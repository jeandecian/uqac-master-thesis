# UQAC Master Thesis

This repository contains the Master's Thesis research conducted by **Jean Decian** for the **University of Quebec at Chicoutimi (UQAC)** in **Master's in Computer Science (3017)**.

**Note:** The final thesis document is written and submitted in **French**; all associated publications are in **English**.

## Associated Publications

This research has resulted in two main publications:

1. **Theoretical Foundation:** [Estimating the Carbon Footprint of Cyberattacks: The Ransomware Case](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-032-05832-4_3) has been published as a chapter in the proceedings of SAFER-TEA 2024 (Springer, Cham).
2. **Empirical Application:** Publication currently under review. **This paper is supported by the datasets in this repository.**

## Structure

* `/code/`: Contains scripts for data cleaning, analysis, and table and figure generation.
* `/data/`: Contains raw and processed datasets.
* `/figures/`: Contains all generated plots and visualizations from the analysis code, including intermediate and final versions.
* `/tables/`: Contains all generated LaTeX tables, including intermediate and final versions.
